Spicy Tuna Onigiri

Brussels sprouts, when cooked correctly, can be one of the tastiest sides for a hearty meal

Ingredients

  • Brussels sprouts
  • Balsamic vinegarette
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Wash and cut the sprouts in halves, and remove the outermost leaves
  2. Toss the sprouts in salt, pepper, and balsamic vinegarette
  3. Add olive oil and heat a pan over medium heat
  4. Add sprouts, and saute
  5. Prior to them being finished, add honey and toss
  6. Remove from heat and enjoy!

Spicy Tuna Onigiri

Delicious sticky rice with a spicy tuna mayo in the center, using some of the cheapest ingredients available, this recipe will fill you up at lunch time!

Ingredients

  • Medium grain rice
  • Canned tuna
  • Mayonnaise
  • Gochujang or Sriracha
  • Dried seaweed (Nori)
  • Toasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Cook the rice! Rice cookers make this easy, or follow these instructions if you don't have access to one
  2. Prepare the Spicy tuna! Mix the mayo, your preferred chilli sauce, and tuna
  3. Divide rice into equal parts and spread evenly into circles
  4. Place a spoon of tuna mixture in the center of the rice
  5. Wet you hands (so the rice doesn't stick to them), and wrap the rice around the spoonful of tuna
  6. Put a strip of nori around the bottom, so you have somewhere to hold the onigiri from without it sticking to your hands!
  7. Garnish the rice balls with a bit of toasted sesame seeds
  8. Enjoy!

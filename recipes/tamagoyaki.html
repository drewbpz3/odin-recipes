Spicy Tuna Onigiri

Tamagoyaki is one of the most popular breakfast dishes in Japan. With a simple to make process, this is easy and affordable as well as delicious!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Soy milk

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients into a bowl
  2. Strain through a sieve a few times to make sure all the portions of egg whites are mixed out. This will make sure it cooks to be fluffy!
  3. Heat a tamagoyaki pan if you have one, or simple use a standard pan (but expect the shape to be a bit off). Add just enough oil to coat the pan
  4. Now time to add the egg. Add just enough to coat the bottom of the pan. pop any bubble that form
  5. Roll the sheet up egg up to one side of the pan. Now add another portion of egg
  6. Roll and repeat until the egg mixture is all used up, typically one roll per egg
  7. Cut into slices and enjoy!
